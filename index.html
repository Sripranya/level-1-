WHY WE'RE HERE...

Our mission? To make sure good food gets eaten, not wasted. Every day, delicious, fresh food goes to waste at functions,
    birthday parties and family parties.The Plate2People lets
donars inform about the surplus food they have and will be able to share their address so that the needy organizations may collect the food from that addres after accepting the donation.

Together, we can be the generation that ends global hunger.
